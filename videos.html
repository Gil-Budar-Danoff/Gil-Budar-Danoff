An earlier film in which I and a group of students were challenged to create stock footage, then assemble unique films. As most of our footage involved some form of violence, I chose to parody old martial arts films.

6 Shots

For this film, I was challenged to create a story with only 6 shots. During this time I was experimenting with 2D animation, though I have not continued down this path. Though the quality of the art may be poor, I am still proud of the story I created, with 6 shots occurring within the story as well as the structure.
